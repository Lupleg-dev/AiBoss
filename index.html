About the Internship:

This opportunity is tailored for those passionate about Software Development and Engineering. As an intern, you'll have the chance to work on cutting-edge projects, enhance your skills, and collaborate with our experienced team. The internship duration is 3 months, and we are looking for students, graduates, and individuals proficient in programming to apply. You should have a basic knowledge of JavaScript, Typescript, Python, and SQL. Familiarity with frameworks and libraries such as Reactjs, Nextjs, Nodejs, Expressjs, and more is also desired. Additionally, proficiency in using tools like Git, Github, VS Code, Postman, and MongoDB Compass is expected. Knowledge of DevOps technologies like AWS, GCP, Docker, and Kubernetes is a plus.

In addition to technical skills, we value good communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and time management skills. The internship offers a collaborative environment where you will learn software development and engineering, work on cutting-edge projects, and enhance your skills. You will have the opportunity to collaborate with an experienced team and gain valuable industry experience.

How to Apply:

If you are ready to embark on a journey of growth and learning, you can fill in once done click the Submit button below. Our team looks forward to reviewing your application and reaching out to qualified candidates. We are transparent therefore even ladies are encouraged to apply.
